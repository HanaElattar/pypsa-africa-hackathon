Welcome to PyPSA meets Africa's documentation!
==============================================

PyPSA meets Africa is a free and open source software project aiming to develop a powerful energy system model for Africa. The tool which is currenlty under development and will be heavily based on `PyPSA https://pypsa.readthedocs.io/en/latest/_` and `PyPSA-Eur https://pypsa-eur.readthedocs.io/en/latest/_`.
